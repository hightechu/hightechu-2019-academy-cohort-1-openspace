INTRODUCTION


Welcome to Open Space!

In Open Space, you play as a pilot flying through an asteriod field.
Your challenge is to make it as far as possible! Good luck!

This game was made in seven weeks, by a team of four teenagers in HighTechU's first cohort of 2019!
Two of those team members continue to maintain and refine the game.

We hope you find this project entertaining!
